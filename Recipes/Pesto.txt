Pesto:

2cups (80g) fresh basil leaves (no stems)
2tablespoons (17g) pine nuts
3large cloves garlic center sections removed (30-45g)
½cup extra-virgin olive oil
½cup freshly grated parmesan cheese


1) Toast pine nuts in a pan over medium-high heat, stirring constantly

2) blast basil leaves, pine nuts, and garlic in a food processor, or blender until finely minced.

3) With the machine running, drizzle the olive oil until the mixture is smooth.

4) Add cheese to the food processor and blast until just combined.

Should be good in the fridge 2-3 days, freezer covered in plastic wrap, probably a couple months
