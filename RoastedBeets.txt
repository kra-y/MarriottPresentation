Roasted Beets:

Ingredients:
4 large peeled beets with greens and root tip removed
2 1/2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Preheat oven to 375 F.

Combine olive oil, salt, pepper in a small bowl. Brush mixture on beets. Place beets in baking dish and cover in aluminum foil.

Bake for 40-45 minutes.
